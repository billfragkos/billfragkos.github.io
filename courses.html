Learn the Fundamentals!

This course is designed for absolute beginners or players who want to reinforce their basics. Chess is 99% tactics, but without fundamentals, tactics are useless. We will build your chess understanding from the ground up.

What you will learn:

  • Complete piece movement and special moves (castling, en passant)
  • Basic checkmates (Queen + King, Rook + King, Two Bishops)
  • Essential tactical motifs (forks, pins, skewers)
  • Simple endgame principles
  • How to analyze your own games

Rock Solid ...c6

The Caro-Kann defense (1.e4 c6) is one of the most respected openings in chess history. This course teaches you how to build an impenetrable fortress and strike back at the right moment.

What you will learn:

  • Complete Caro-Kann repertoire for Black
  • How to handle the Advance, Exchange, and Classical variations
  • Typical pawn structures and middlegame plans
  • Counterattacking ideas against White's aggressive setups
  • Model games from Caro-Kann masters
