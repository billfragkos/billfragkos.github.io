About Me

Get to know the person behind the board

Vasileios Fragkos

Who I am

My name is Vasileios Fragkos, and chess has been my passion for over 15 years. What started as a childhood hobby quickly became a lifelong pursuit of excellence, strategy, and continuous learning. I've competed in numerous national and international tournaments, achieving a FIDE rating of 2200+.

My Coaching Philosophy

I believe that every student has unique strengths. My approach combines classical chess principles with modern analytical tools. I don't just teach moves — I teach how to think like a grandmaster. Patience, pattern recognition, and psychological resilience are at the core of my method.

Achievements

  • 2nd place – Greek National Championship (2018)
  • FIDE Instructor Certificate (2020)
  • Peak rating: 2245 FIDE Classical
  • 200+ students coached to rating improvements

Beyond the Board

When I'm not teaching or playing, I study chess psychology, analyze historical games, and contribute to chess forums. I'm also an advocate for using chess as a tool for cognitive development in young learners.
